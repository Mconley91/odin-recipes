Spaghetti

Description:

A tomato sauce based noodle dish seasoned with italian herbs and often served with meatballs and mozzraella.

The dish is named after the kind of noodles usually used.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Olive or Vegetable Oil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Italian Herbs
  • Ground Meat or Meatballs (optional)

Steps:

  1. Boil large pot of water
  2. Add uncooked spaghetti noodles to boiling water, and remove from heat. Keep the pot lid on.
  3. Heat a skillet to ~300F
  4. Add oil & meat to hot skillet, season with herbs to taste, stirr & turn periodically
  5. After 10 minutes: drain pot of hot water & now cooked spaghetti noodles into straining colander
  6. Place straining colander into now empty but still warm pot and apply a modest amount of oil to cooked noodles, stir oil into noodles to prevent sticking.
  7. Add tomato sauce & tomato paste to mostly cooked meat in hot skillet, continue stirring to ensure even doneness & prevent burning.
  8. After meat is cooked, turn off heat.
  9. Serve sauce and meat over cooked & unsauced spaghetti noodles
  10. Add shredded mozzraella and spices to taste
