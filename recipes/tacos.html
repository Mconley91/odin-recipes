Tacos

Description:

Corn or flour tortillas paired with cooked meats, vegetables, and sauces.

Ingredients:

  • Corn or Flour tortillas
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Meats of choice
  • Vegetables of choice
  • Southwest spices (cumin, chili powder, salt, etc)

Steps:

  1. Oil & heat skillet to ~300F
  2. Add meat of choice, season generously and turn as needed to prevent burning and ensure even cook
  3. When meat is partially done, add vegetables, mix, and season again
  4. Warm tortillas in oven or skillet at a low heat
  5. When meat is fully cooked and vegetables are slightly soft, turn off heat
  6. Serve meat & vegetables together in warm tortilla, add sauce, cheese, or fresh vegetables as desired
